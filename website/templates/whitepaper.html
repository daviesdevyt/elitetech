White paper


Introduction

As the world continues to grow digital over the years, the future of cryptocurrencies and blockchain is growing rapidly, Macro Token is the first cryptocurrency to be incorporated in everyday games, it is a play to earn cryptocurrency and it also in cooperates mining on the cloud.

Blockchain technology has made it possible to decentralize almost everything, Macro Token (MCR) is a BEP20 token on Binance smart Chain (BSC) network, the MCR token is used on the website for gaming, you play games each day to win MCR, on the website you can also mine MCR every 24hrs which also increases with each referral you obtain. The MCR also has a betting section, where you stake crypto on a condition that you have in mind.


Vision

To Build a platform secured and operated by everyday people that in cooperates cryptocurrency into games. Build the World largest game mine Cryptocurrency, to be played by everyone, with everyone having fun and making profit in cryptocurrencies.
